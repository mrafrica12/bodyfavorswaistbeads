Our Story

"They are a secret piece of jewelry that help women feel feminine and sexy."

Body Favors Waist Beads was born from a deep love for African tradition and a belief that every woman deserves to feel beautiful in her own skin. Based in Atlanta, GA, we handcraft each bead strand with intention — because the act of adorning your body is sacred.

Regardless of your reason for wearing beads, we believe they should be worn respectfully and joyfully. Whether you're tracking your body's journey, setting daily affirmations, or simply feeling yourself — we're honored to be on that journey with you.

The Process

How It Works

Getting started with waist beads is easier than you think. Here's how to make them yours.

1

Choose Your Strand

Browse our collection and select the colors that speak to you. Each color carries meaning — let your intuition guide you. Not sure? Our Color Chart page explains every hue.

2

Wrap & Tie

Wrap the strand around your waist at your desired position. Tie a double knot, trim the excess, and you're done. Our How To Tie guide has everything you need — step by step.

3

Wear with Intention

Set your affirmation, feel your body, and let the beads remind you daily of your beauty and power. Wear them in the shower, in the gym, in bed — they move with you, always.
