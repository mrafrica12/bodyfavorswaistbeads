Shipping Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1–2 business days (Monday–Friday, excluding holidays) from our Atlanta, GA studio. You will receive an email confirmation once your order has been placed, and a separate shipping confirmation with tracking information once your order ships.

Shipping Methods & Rates

We currently ship within the United States. Shipping rates are calculated at checkout based on your location and order weight.

  • Standard Shipping (3–5 business days): Rates calculated at checkout. FREE on orders over $55!
  • Expedited Shipping (2–3 business days): Available at an additional cost, calculated at checkout.
  • Priority Overnight: Available for select locations. Contact us before ordering to confirm availability.

Free Shipping

All orders over $55 qualify for free standard shipping within the continental United States. Use code FREESHIP at checkout, or the discount will be applied automatically when your cart reaches $55.

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can track your package directly on the carrier's website (USPS or UPS). Please allow 24 hours for tracking information to update after your shipping confirmation email.

Lost or Damaged Packages

If your package is lost in transit or arrives damaged, please contact us within 7 days of the expected delivery date. We will work with the carrier to investigate and resolve the issue. Please do not discard any damaged packaging, as we may request photos.

Returns & Exchanges

Due to the intimate nature of waist beads and hygiene considerations, we do not accept returns on worn items. Unused, unopened items may be returned within 14 days of delivery in original condition. Please contact us before sending any items back.

If you received the wrong item or your order is defective, please contact us immediately with your order number and photos. We will make it right.

International Shipping

We currently ship within the United States only. International shipping is in development — sign up for our newsletter to be notified when we expand to your country.

Contact Us

Have a question about your order? Reach us at hello@bodyfavorswaistbeads.com or through our Contact page.
