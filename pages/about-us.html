Our Story

Where It Began

Born from Love of Self

Body Favors Waist Beads began with a single belief: every woman deserves to feel beautiful in her own skin — at every size, at every stage. Inspired by the rich waist bead traditions of West and Central Africa, we started handcrafting bead strands for ourselves, then our friends, then the world.

Based in Atlanta, GA, every product we make is crafted with intention. We select our beads for quality, color meaning, and energy. We string each strand by hand. We ship each order with a personal touch. This is not mass production — this is devotion.

Whether you're wearing beads for the first time or you've been adorning your body for decades, we are honored to be part of your practice.
